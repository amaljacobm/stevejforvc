ABOUT STEVE JORDAN

    Steve is a dedicated advocate and catalyst for change who relentlessly fights against workplace discrimination. He secured wage increases and provided invaluable support to hundreds of members during the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic. He has received accolades for his contributions to community development and youth engagement; empowering others by helping to build a brighter and more equitable future within the workplace and the community. Steve's professional journey stands as a testament to what can be achieved through dedication, resilience, and an unwavering commitment to positive change.

ACHIEVEMENTS

  • In my 38 years of service, I have demonstrated unwavering commitment to representing workers’ concerns and achieving positive results.
  • Extensive knowledge of the Collective Agreements, union operations, and the issues that matter most to members.
  • Have fought for countless members, successfully resolving issues related to workers’ rights, lack of access, and unfair treatment.
  • Unparalleled insight into the struggles and aspirations of members across various City departments and divisions.
  • Believe in collaboration and transparency, and value your input.
  • Have a forward-thinking plan for union growth and improved benefits.

    I am fully committed to working tirelessly for a more equitable and inclusive workplace for all CUPE Local 79 members.

FUTURE GOALS

  • Ensure every member’s opinion holds significance, regardless of their background and identity.
  • Oversee equity initiatives that support a culture of inclusion in the workplace.
  • Promote equitable access to resources and opportunities for every member.
  • Provide swift resolution of grievances and ensure fair treatment for members across all bargaining units.
  • Work to improve member wages and benefits.
  • Support diverse and underrepresented member groups to grow into leadership roles.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional union groups and committees to resolve members’ concerns.
  • Use member feedback to make informed choices that enhance our collective progress.

    Together, we will create a fairer, stronger union. Let's build a better future together!
